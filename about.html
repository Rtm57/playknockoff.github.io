About

Read all about us!

Knock-Off is a 3D, competitive, multiplayer, action-strategy game in which players use directionally constrained abilities to force their enemies off an ever-deteriorating map.
Players gather “spells” and cast them in a direction to either push or pull enemies or shatter the ground to create holes in the map.
These abilities allow for complex and interesting situations, forcing players to constantly re-position themselves and create new strategies on the fly in the effort to “knock off” their enemies while also trying to stay alive.

Use Fire, Earth, & Water to Secure Your Victory!

  • Knock your opponents off the arena for a sweet and satisfying victory!
  • Craft your spell kit through your unique rotational inventory and discover combos to crush your enemies!
  • Gather for an epic battle in one of three unique arenas revealing detail and story about its people!
  • Choose to fight as your favorite character hailing from their regional homeland!
  • Host LAN parties with your friends to hone your skills and keep the fun going!
