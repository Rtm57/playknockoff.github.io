    Description

    Knock-Off is a 3D, competitive, multiplayer, action-strategy game in which players use directionally constrained abilities to force their enemies off an ever-deteriorating map. Players gather “spells” and cast them in a direction to either push or pull enemies or shatter the ground to create holes in the map.
    These abilities allow for complex and interesting situations, forcing players to constantly re-position themselves and create new strategies on the fly in the effort to “knock off” their enemies while also trying to stay alive.

    History

    Knock-Off Games was formed in the late Summer months of 2019. The attractiveness for Knock-Off was simple: to eliminate your enemies off the arena, but to do so through the spell kit-crafting aspect of the rotational inventory system. This provided a unique gameplay experience where players are encouraged to specialize into their own personal playstyle while still retaining the goal of staying alive and eliminating enemies in mind.
    Soon enough, many parties were interested in the game concept and its visual style potential. Over time, Knock-Off Games grew from a team size of four (4) to fifteen (15) team members with various disciplines ranging from game design, visual art, programming, and music.

    Features

  • Knock your opponents off the arena for a sweet and satisfying victory!
  • Craft your spell kit through your unique rotational inventory and discover combos to crush your enemies!
  • Gather for an epic battle in one of three unique arenas revealing detail and story about its people!
  • Choose to fight as your favorite character hailing from their regional homeland!
  • Host LAN parties with your friends to hone your skills and keep the fun going!
