Spells & Game Modes

Learn more about the various spells and game modes available in Knock-Off!

Spells

    Water - Pulls enemies towards you with a powerful current. 30 ft. range.
    Fire - Blasts enemies away from you with a mighty fireball. 30 ft. range.
    Earth - Destroy the ground in front of you with a powerful quake. 40 ft. range.

Storage System

In Knockoff, players don’t have to cast their spells immediately on pickup. The Triad Storage System allows players to store their spells into one of three spaces, called Trines, for use at a later time. The Trine that the spell will be alloted to is dependent on the player’s direction when picking up the spell.

Gamemodes

  • Elimination - Each player spawns with a set number of lives. The last player standing wins!
  • Deathmatch - The player with the most knockouts at the end of the match wins!

Game Strats

    General Combos
  • Water > Fire: Use water to bring opponents closer to the ledge, then blast them with fire to send them off of the stage!
  • Earth > Water: Destroy the tiles behind you with earth, then use water to pull opponents into the gap!
  • Earth > Fire: Use earth to trap your opponents, then follow up with a fireball!
    Super Combos
  • Collect the same spell in every Trine and use Dash to unleash a Super Combo!
    • Fire: Pushes all opponents in a 15 ft. radius away from you.
    • Water: Pulls all opponents in a 15 ft. radius towards you.
    • Earth: Replaces destroyed tiles instantly and Roots all opponents caught within its radius
